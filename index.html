Arda Turan

Turkish football manager

Arda Turan is a retired Turkish footballer born on January 30, 1987, in Istanbul. He primarily played as a winger or attacking midfielder. Turan began his professional career at Galatasaray, later moving to Atlético Madrid in 2011, where he won La Liga and reached the UEFA Champions League final. In 2015, he signed with FC Barcelona, adding multiple domestic titles to his name. Known for his creativity, vision, and technical skills, Turan also earned over 100 caps for the Turkish national team. He returned to Galatasaray before retiring in 2022. Post-retirement, Turan has pursued a career in football management.
